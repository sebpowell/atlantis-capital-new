Excellence through the science of investing

Atlantis Capital Management is an independent investment management firm and multi family office authorised and regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority. We specialize in the creation of bespoke investment solutions and full wealth management offering designed to achieve our clients’ objectives.

Mission & Values

Our mission is to provide a holistic value-added approach towards wealth management characterized by strong adherence to the values upon which Atlantis was built.

Ethos

We pride ourselves in adhering to the highest ethical code of conduct in the industry. Our platform is specifically designed to remove conflicts of interest; allowing us to truly partner with our clients.

Transparency

Atlantis operates on an open architecture approach which enables clients to benefit from a complete alignment of interests and full flexibility around product selection and the construction of investment propositions.

Expertise

Our clients directly benefit from the knowledge and experience of our investment advisers and research team. All personnel hail from top-tier institutions and represent the highest calibre of personal integrity and investment proficiency.

Innovation & Excellence

Atlantis has an in-house research and risk team established to provide accurate risk management tools and market analysis techniques. This independence allows us to deliver a truly bespoke experience when it comes to deciphering markets and engineering investments designed according to clients’ risk appetite and investment objectives.

Wealth Advisory

At Atlantis we provide our clients with lifestyle planning and wealth management services adapted to their individual circumstances. We appreciate that every situation is different, as such we emphasize the needs for a deep understanding of our clients’ situation.

This will enable us to develop targeted and customized solutions designed to achieve their objectives. The global nature of Atlantis allows us to advise international clients as well as to construct complex asset and enterprise structures.

Investment Management

Our investment management offering is unique and transparent. Clients have the option of selecting either a discretionary or an advisory mandate with us. All investment solutions are entirely bespoke; taking into account the personal circumstances and risk appetite of our clients.

Portfolio construction is tailored to achieve the investment objectives of our clients and processes are constantly monitored and evaluated to ensure the advice given is relevant and appropriate.

  • Tactical Trading

    Our internal proprietary technology allows us to identify profitable market dislocations which can be capitalised on.

  • Portfolio Consolidation

    We provide our advisers with the information needed to construct a well-diversified portfolio and to ensure optimized allocation.

  • Portfolio Construction

    Our team works closely with the client’s adviser to construct the best possible portfolio given the client’s risk tolerance and investment objectives.

  • Structured Solutions

    We create bespoke structured solutions designed for participation, hedging purposes or income generation.

  • Alternative Investments

    We provide investment opportunities in alternative asset classes, such as private equity, venture capital, off-market opportunities, real estate, art and club deals.

  • Performance Reporting

    We enable our clients to gauge exactly where the risk lies and what factors have contributed the most to the performance of the investments.
