The science of investing

At Atlantis we believe in turning the ‘art of investing’ into the science of investing. We make use of quantitative finance techniques to aid in the construction of a more precise and tailored investment approach for clients. Our research and risk team is constantly developing better tactical hedging models and alpha generation strategies that enable us to create a sustainable edge by identifying mispricing in the market and through the creation of absolute return strategies.

The quant approach is also a key tool in portfolio optimization, risk monitoring and rebalancing. This approach helps our team actively monitor and rebalance asset allocations as the needs of our clients change and as market conditions alter.

  • Risk Control

    The quantitative approach means we can justify exactly how we generate investment ideas.
